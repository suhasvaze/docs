# 2. Streams / Tracks

What are various Streams / Tracks within Fusion?

View on Oracle Website   (opens new window)

# Enterprise Resource Planning

ERP

  1. Financial Management
  2. Procurement
  3. Project Management
  4. Risk Management and Compliance
  5. Enterprise Performance Management

















# Financials

Oracle Cloud Financials

E.g. Accounting from Subledgers(AP/AR/FA etc), AR Invoices, AR Receipts, AP Invoices, AP Payments, Expenses, Collections, Cash Clearing/Reconciliation, Reporting etc

  1. General Ledger (GL)
  2. Accounts Payable and Expenses (AP)
  3. Accounts Receivable, Collections and Advanced Collections(AR)
  4. Cash Management (CE)
  5. Assets and Lease Management (FA)
  6. Accounting Hub Cloud (AHC)
  7. Subledger Accounting (SLA)

🚀 Career Option: Major option for CAs, CMAs, Commerce Graduates, MBAs (Fin) etc

















# Procurement

Oracle Cloud Procurement

Requisition → Purchase Order → (SCM Material/Service Receipt) → ( FINANCIALS AP Invoice → AP Payment → Clearing Reconciliation )

  1. Supplier Management
  2. Sourcing
  3. Procurement Contracts
  4. Purchasing (Procure To Pay)
  5. Direct Procurement
  6. Procurement Analytics

💡 Career Option: for Engineers, Materials & Logistics Professionals etc

















# Projects

Oracle Project Management

Management, Costing and Billing of Large Projects

  1. Project Planning and Scheduling
  2. Project Costing
  3. Project Billing and Revenue Management
  4. Project Asset Management
  5. Grant Management
  6. Program Management and Reporting

💡 Career Option: for CAs, CMAs, Commerce Graduates, MBAs (Fin) etc

















# Risk Management and Compliance

RMC

Control Secured User Access, Segregation of Duties

  1. User Access Control and Monitoring (with Automation)
  2. User Activity Monitoring (Continuous)
  3. Reporting and Compliance (Audit/SOX)

💡 Career Option: for CAs, CMAs, Commerce Graduates, MBAs (Fin) etc

















# Enterprise Perf. Management

EPM - Enterprise Performance Management

Streamlining Financials Close Process, Profit and Cost Analysis

  1. Profitability and Cost Management
  2. Consolidation
  3. Reconciliation
  4. Tax Reporting

💡 Career Option: for CAs, CMAs, Commerce Graduates, MBAs (Fin) etc

















# Supply Chain Management

SCM

  1. Supply Chain Planning - Plan Demand and Supply
  2. Inventory Management - Inventory Levels, Costing, and Materials Management
  3. Manufacturing - Discreet and Process Manufacturing, Production Scheduling
  4. Maintenance - Asset Maintenance Scheduling and Work Execution
  5. Order Management - Sales Orders, Pricing, Promising, Revenue Management
  6. Logistics - Warehouse Management, Transport Management
  7. Product Lifecycle Management - Product Development, Product Quality , Product Master Data
  8. More SCM applications - SCM Analytics

💡 Career Option: for Engineers, Materials & Logistics Professionals

















# Customer Experience

CX (earlier CRM - Customer Relationship Management)

  1. Marketing - Campaigns and Lead Generation
  2. Sales - Customer Data Management, Sales Planning and Performance Mgmt
  3. Service - Customer Self Service, Agent Assisted Service, Field Service

💡 Career Option: for Sales and Marketing Professionals
















# Human Capital Management

HCM (earlier HRMS - Human Resource Management System)

  1. Core Human Resources - Employee Information, Planning, Benefits, HCM Controls
  2. Talent Management - Recruiting, Onboarding, Learning, Career Dev, Succession
  3. Workforce Management - Scheduling, Optimization, Absence, Time and Labor
  4. Payroll - Core Payroll, Payroll Interface (Transfer), Payroll Connect (Info)

💡 Career Option: for Human Capital Professionals
















# Approval Management

AMX

Configure Approvals for Purchase Orders, Payables Invoices, Journals etc


💡 Career Option: Added skill for any other Stream / Application
